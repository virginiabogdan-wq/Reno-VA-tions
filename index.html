About Virginia

Creativity and practicality, in equal measure

"Much of my work centers around repurposing and reimagining existing materials, bringing new life to what already exists."

I'm drawn to DIY projects that create functional, personalized spaces, ones that reflect how people actually live. Whether I'm refinishing furniture, transforming a found piece, or designing a custom build, the goal is the same: something that feels both meaningful and intentional.

What I care about most is the part you don't always see. The process, the storytelling, and the problem-solving behind each transformation are as much the point as the finished result.
